Graduation Project: Eagle Eye

The project focus in helping to enhance security and surveillance system as it helps in searching for suspects when crime happens and the specific time of its occurrence is unknown. The project consists of two flows flow one : when we have an image of the suspect instead of looking through multiple long cctv video(s) to find them our system takes an image of the suspect and the video(s) and search for them and it returns the frame they were found in with the time stamp. flow two : when we don't have an image of the suspect the system take the cctv video(s) and it could be trimmed to a wanted range and it will return all the frames where faces are detected with their time stamps.
